# Hi, my name is Illia

## Who am I? 

I am a Senior Frontend Engineer with 5 years of experience.

I liked computers from a young age, I was fascinated by how they worked and how they could be used to solve problems. I was never scared to go inside the hardware and take a closer look at what was going on. 

At some point in life, I was deciding to go to Frontend or Backend Development, and I chose the Frontend path. I learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and started building my first web applications. Then I heard about ReactJS at this [talk](https://youtu.be/R4sTvHXkToQ) by [Sanya Solovyov](https://github.com/sansolovyov/), and I decided to take a look. 

**Long story short:** 
I started my career as an intern at [Zaelab](https://www.zaelab.com/) in e-commerce practice, in a group that was working with an e-commerce technology called VTEX. I was learning and working with ReactJS and VTEX. That was a chance to learn and work with a modern frontend framework.

After that, I was moved to the group working on the [Threekit](https://www.threekit.com/) platform, integrating the Threekit platform with the React application and powering them with [Logik.io](https://www.logik.io/) technology.

And for this time I am working on this type of project, building composable UIs not only with Threekit, but with kBridge as 3d/2d renderer and Paradigm as a back-office technology. 

Throughout the time, I built a lot of templates and internal tools to speed up and simplify the development process for the team.
